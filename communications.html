Group Process and Communications

Overview of how our group will keep in touch and communicate effectively with each other

Communications between group members will be split into 3 platforms based on the information wanting to be shared. Our chatting platform for immediate response will be done through Discord as it is compatible on PC and Mobile and gives notifications to when someone posts anything in it. Bi-weekly face-to-face meetings will be done via MS Teams for the ease of recording meetings with a transcript to be reflected upon later if needed. Microsoft SharePoint will be used to share, store, and collate all our files in a cloud storage system that is both safe and secure and easily accessible and editable by every member of the group with changes made being saved for others to see. Update reports, either in the face-to-face meetings or over Discord, from every member will be required at least every few days with a group member, Lachlan Wild, in charge of making sure everyone is communicating and constantly updating the rest of the team of their work. Any friction between members will also be addressed and handled appropriately by the entire group led by Kevin Sweeney as to stamp out friction and bad blood from the root early and not let it spread throughout the project.
