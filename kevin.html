I was born and raised in the Kimberley region of Western Australia where I have lived and worked for various periods of my life. I currently work full-time as store manager of the local hardware store. I have always had a love for technology and have a penchant for science fiction movies and television shows. I gained an interest in IT (Information Technology) ever since I first saw a personal computer and developed during high school. Living in the Kimberley, I do occasionally go four-wheel-driving and camping when time and weather allow. Fishing is big in the top-end, but I am not really into it.
