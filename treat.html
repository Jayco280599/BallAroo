Treat Dispenser

One feature of the BallAroo to encourage usage by the intended clientele of the product, is the option to dispense treats during use. This would require an in-built hopper where the treats would be stored within the unit, that would be accessed via a pet-proof lid. A dispenser mechanism would have to developed to accommodate the large variety of pet treats available on the market yet be simple enough to maintain reliability and reduced costs. The most likely candidate would be a rotating drum type dispenser that is capable of handling pellet type pet treats, simple in construction, easy to control and cheap to manufacture. The output of the treat dispenser will be low as possible to the ground and be of a design to accommodate as many of the dog breeds in existence.
