Power

Power for the BallAroo would be provided by an on-board lithium-ion battery. The use of lithium-ion battery would allow the unit to be powered for extended time periods, while remaining portable and light enough for owners of the unit to transport it to areas of intended use with ease. The battery size coupled with power efficiency measures in the software would be intended to achieve 20-30mins on high power ball launches and greater than 45mins on short to medium launch settings. It would not be unrealistic to achieve over 60mins of runtime when limited to short low power ball launches. Though overall real-world runtime would be down to settings selected by the user and activity levels of the dog using the product. Once energy consumption is established and battery size determined, then the size of the power-supply can be decided on. Selection of an off-the-shelf power supply meeting the voltage and watt requirements would ensure in keeping the overall costs of the product as low as possible.
