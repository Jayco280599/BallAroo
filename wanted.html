Wanted Members

If Spark 22 was approached by an interested investor who want to finance the development of the BallAroo product. If said investor was willing to provide funding for up to four additional team members, these would be the most likely positions Spark 22 would seek:

Mechatronics Engineer

The mechatronics engineer would be instrumental in designing the core working components of the BallAroo. From the ball feed unit, ball throw, turn table and treat dispenser, these components would fall under the responsibilities of the mechatronics engineer.

Key Responsibilities Include:

  • Design, develop and improve mechatronic devices from concept to reality.
  • Collaborate with team members to develop, design, and document parts and final products.
  • Identify areas of potential improvements to product design.
  • Assist in the manufacture of prototypes and proof of design concepts.
  • Assist in selection of materials and required tooling for product manufacturing.

The ideal candidate would process most of the following:

  • Recognised certification in Mechatronics Engineering, Advanced Electronics and Robotics.
  • Industry experience in mechatronics and control systems.
  • Certifications/Experience in CAD / Solid works and similar software.
  • Experience in PCB design and manufacture.
  • Excellent problem solving and analytical skills.
  • Knowledge of computer hardware functionality and architecture.
  • Self-motivated and open to new ideas and learning.

Software Developer / Full-Stack Engineer

The software engineer/full-stack engineer would be brought onboard to start building the supporting cloud infrastructure required for the project. This would include the project/product website and any cloud-based infrastructure that the project requires. The required cloud infrastructure would continue to will evolve as the project moves along to producing shipping product. The software engineer would also be tasked in developing mobile app for the project if the need arises.

Key Responsibilities Include:

  • Facilitate in design, specification, implementation and testing of cloud infrastructure.
  • Assist in integration of products with cloud infrastructure.
  • Being proactive regarding systems security and privacy requirements of customers.

The ideal candidate would process most of the following:

  • Degree in Software Engineering or Computer Science.
  • Solid experience with HTML 5, CSS, JavaScript and Angular.
  • Experience working with databases.
  • Proficient working with Git and GitHub.

Desirable Skills:

  • Objective-C, C++ and JAVA programming.
  • iPhone and Android app development experience.

Industrial Designer / Fabricator

The industrial designer/fabricator would assist the mechatronics engineer with the design and manufacture of the components required for the prototypes. As the project moves from proof-of-concept prototypes to production prototypes, the industrial designer would be responsible for the final appearance of the product.

Key Responsibilities Include:

  • Assist in the design, visualisation and manufacture of product prototypes.
  • Collaborate with team members to develop design document parts and final products.
  • Identify areas of potential improvements to product design.
  • Assist in production of marketing materials and website design.
  • Liaising with manufacturing partners.
  • Assist in selection of materials and required tooling for product manufacturing.

The idea candidate would process most of the following:

  • Experience / Certifications in Industrial Design.
  • Solid experience with 3D CAD Modelling.
  • Proficient working with CNC equipment and 3D Printers.
  • Self-motivated and open to new ideas and learning.

Sales Representative / Marketing Manager

Sales representative/marketing manager would be required as the project gets ready to start manufacture of the final product. The sale representative / marketing manager would help build relationships with possible retail partners. The sales representative/marketing manager would also be responsible for devising and driving the marketing campaign, building mind share amongst potential customers in the target market for the product.

Responsibilities Include:

  • Building market presence.
  • Developing of company and product branding.
  • Planning and implementation of marketing campaigns.
  • Driving product sales.
  • Maintaining re-seller relationships.

The successful applicant would process the following:

  • Certifications and/or experience in Marketing.
  • Previous Sales experience.
  • Have excellent interpersonal skills.
  • Excellent Communication skills.
  • Confidant personality.
  • Good to excellent computer literacy (MS Office suite).
